Leadership Studies & Ethics

Developing principled leaders who inspire and act with integrity under pressure.

Course Overview

This course explores the theories and practices of effective leadership, emphasizing ethical decision-making and moral responsibility. Students will engage with historical and contemporary leadership models, learn to navigate complex moral dilemmas, and cultivate the character and skills necessary to lead with clarity and courage.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand key leadership theories and frameworks
  • Analyze ethical challenges faced by leaders in high-stakes environments
  • Apply ethical decision-making models to real-world scenarios
  • Develop personal leadership philosophy grounded in integrity
  • Enhance communication and conflict resolution skills

Course Outline (Week by Week)

  • Week 1: Foundations of Leadership Theory
  • Week 2: Leadership Styles and Adaptability
  • Week 3: Ethics in Leadership – Principles and Frameworks
  • Week 4: Moral Philosophy and Decision-Making Models
  • Week 5: Case Studies: Ethical Dilemmas in Leadership
  • Week 6: Communication Strategies for Leaders
  • Week 7: Conflict Management and Negotiation
  • Week 8: Building and Leading Effective Teams
  • Week 9: Leadership under Pressure and Crisis Management
  • Week 10: Developing Personal Leadership Philosophy
  • Week 11: Leadership Ethics in a Global Context
  • Week 12: Final Project: Leadership Case Analysis and Presentation

Evaluation Criteria

  • Weekly Reflections: Critical analyses of leadership concepts and ethical issues
  • Midterm Paper: Ethical dilemma case study
  • Final Project: Comprehensive leadership case analysis and presentation
  • Participation: Class discussions, peer feedback, and group activities

Mini Glossary

Term Definition
Leadership The act of guiding and influencing individuals or groups toward a goal.
Ethics The moral principles governing behavior and decision-making.
Moral Dilemma A situation in which a difficult choice must be made between competing ethical principles.
Transformational Leadership A leadership style focused on inspiring and motivating change.
Transactional Leadership Leadership based on exchanges, rewards, and punishments.
Integrity Adherence to ethical and moral principles; honesty.
Conflict Resolution Methods to resolve disputes and manage disagreements effectively.
Decision-Making Model Frameworks that help analyze and choose among alternatives.
