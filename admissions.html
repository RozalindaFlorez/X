Admissions Process

  1. Initial Submission: Complete the online application and statement of intent.
  2. Evaluation Round: We assess academic records, clarity of purpose, and personal rigor.
  3. Situational Interview: Conducted virtually with real-time decision-making challenges.
  4. Immersion Weekend: Finalists are brought to campus for a 48-hour full-spectrum engagement.

Candidates are evaluated not for perfection, but for precision, command, and potential. We seek individuals who act with clarity and adapt under pressure.

What We Look For

  • Strategic Intelligence – Ability to synthesize, lead, and anticipate.
  • Inner Discipline – A drive for mastery that is self-directed and unrelenting.
  • Resilience – Psychological durability in high-stakes environments.
  • Team Integrity – A deep sense of contribution and leadership without ego.

Tuition & Financial Aid

The cost of attending X Veritas Collegiate reflects the depth, rigor, and rarity of the education you receive. Tuition for the 2026–2027 academic year is $19,700. This includes academic programming, immersive modules, and field simulations.

Financial Aid

Over 60% of cadets receive financial aid. Aid is awarded based on merit, need, and demonstrated potential for leadership under pressure. We offer:

  • Merit Scholarships – Awarded for elite performance in academics and evaluation.
  • Need-Based Aid – Determined via FAFSA and supplemental financial profiles.
  • Mission Grants – For cadets entering underserved fields or service sectors.
