Course Overview

This course immerses students in advanced principles of engineering and technology critical for developing resilient, innovative systems. It covers topics ranging from materials science and robotics to emerging tech applications in defense and infrastructure.

Cadets will engage in hands-on labs, design challenges, and real-world problem solving to build proficiency in technical innovation and strategic application.

Learning Objectives

  • Master core engineering principles across mechanical, electrical, and systems domains
  • Develop proficiency in robotics and automation technologies
  • Analyze and apply advanced materials for strategic advantage
  • Integrate emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and cybersecurity into engineering solutions
  • Lead design projects from concept through prototyping and testing

Course Outline (Week by Week)

  • Week 1: Fundamentals of Advanced Engineering Principles
  • Week 2: Materials Science and Structural Analysis
  • Week 3: Robotics Systems and Automation
  • Week 4: Electrical Systems and Embedded Technologies
  • Week 5: Artificial Intelligence in Engineering Applications
  • Week 6: Internet of Things (IoT) for Strategic Operations
  • Week 7: Cybersecurity Principles in Engineering Systems
  • Week 8: Design Thinking and Innovation Methodologies
  • Week 9: Prototyping and Testing Labs
  • Week 10: Project Management and Team Leadership
  • Week 11: Case Studies in Advanced Engineering Solutions
  • Week 12: Final Design Project and Presentation

Evaluation Criteria

  • Lab Assignments: Hands-on engineering tasks and reports
  • Midterm Exam: Theoretical knowledge and application
  • Final Design Project: Comprehensive engineering solution presentation
  • Participation: Team collaboration and discussion contributions

Mini Glossary

Term Definition
Robotics The design, construction, and operation of robots for automated tasks.
Automation Technology that reduces human intervention by automating processes.
Materials Science The study of material properties and their applications in engineering.
Embedded Systems Specialized computing systems integrated into devices and machinery.
Internet of Things (IoT) Network of interconnected devices exchanging data and enabling smart operations.
Cybersecurity Measures taken to protect systems from digital attacks and threats.
Design Thinking A problem-solving approach emphasizing user-centered innovation.
Prototyping Creating preliminary models to test and refine design concepts.
